Our Philosophy

Made With Love

At Luna Piena, food is an expression of care. Our menu features homemade dishes inspired by Colombian, Italian and Venezuelan cuisine and comfort food classics, prepared fresh upon request.

From fluffy arepas in the morning to comforting pasta in the evening, every meal is crafted to make you feel at home—nourished and happy.

Our special menu is available upon request. Please let us know your preferences and we'll prepare something delicious for you.
