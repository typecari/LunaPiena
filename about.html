Who We Are

Our Story

Born From a Dream of Italian Hospitality

Luna Piena by Lupo is a boutique hotel experience that blends Italian charm with a modern, thoughtful touch. Each stay is carefully designed to feel like a full-moon night: peaceful, welcoming, and unforgettable.

We focus on creating an atmosphere of connection and comfort, where every detail is crafted to leave a lasting impression. From the moment you arrive, you become part of our family.

Our Philosophy

Clean, Peaceful, Thoughtful

Our spaces are designed to inspire calm and connection, offering a timeless retreat from the everyday. We believe in the power of simplicity—that the most memorable experiences come from genuine warmth, not excess.

Every element of Luna Piena reflects this vision, from our carefully curated interiors to the personalized service that anticipates your every need.
